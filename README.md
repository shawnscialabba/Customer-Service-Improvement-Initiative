# Customer-Service-Improvement-Initiative

### Summary

Managed a project focused on enhancing the customer service experience and improving operational efficiency.

### Scope & Implementation

*   Implemented a new customer relationship management (CRM) system.
*   Developed and delivered training programs for customer service representatives.
*   Analyzed customer feedback to identify pain points and implement solutions.

### Results & Impact

*   **Customer Satisfaction:** Increased customer satisfaction scores by 15%.
*   **Resolution Time:** Reduced average customer issue resolution time by 10%.
*   **Efficiency:** Improved team efficiency through streamlined workflows and better use of technology.

*   Customer Service | Process Improvement | CRM Systems | Training & Development | Data Analysis | Team Leadership
